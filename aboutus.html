Our team

Aniruddh Bhaskaran


I was a fan of all types of sports from a young age.Football, squash, table tennis : you name it, I've probably played it. Now my most favorite sport is basketball, and I am crazy about it!

Samaksh Goel


I started playing cricket when I was around 3 years old. I still remember what my answer was when people asked me what my interests were. A lot has changed but that one thing that didn’t change is the answer to that very question i.e. Cricket. That one answer has stayed consistent for the past 13 years even though a lot had been added to that. I am very curious about every sport I play and have learnt something from every sport. Every sport teaches us something or the other at different times and at different levels. The higher we go, the more experience we gain and that is what life is about. “Sport Teaches you character, It teaches you to play by the rules, it teaches you to know what it feels like to win and lose, It teaches you about Life.”

Baalateja Kataru


I have been playing football since I was 10. I've trained in multiple coaching centres and I've played in many teams. I am very passionate about it, I believe football is the one thing that helps me take my mind of things and concentrate only on the ball. It unites everyone and makes us forget all our problems. Whenever I feel sad or depressed, I start to do something with a football and that feeling goes away. I am a hardcore Real Madrid fan. I always had a dream to host a football tournament, and this is my dream come true

Aman Saxena

Niranjan Sridhar
